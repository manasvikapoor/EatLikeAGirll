BURRITO RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 6 ounces sliced jalapeno peppers
  2. 1 tomato, diced
  3. 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
  4. 1 green bell pepper, diced
  5. 1 red bell pepper, diced
  6. 1 onion, diced
  7. 1 1/2 tablespoons hot sauce
  8. 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  9. 1 pound ground beef
  10. 1 (1 ounce) package burrito seasoning
  11. 1 (14 ounce) can refried beans
  12. 6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  13. 1 (10 ounce) bag shredded lettuce
  14. 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  15. 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix jalapeno peppers, tomato, green chile peppers, green bell pepper, red bell pepper in a bowl.
  2. Then add onion, hot sauce, and cayenne pepper together in the bowl.
  3. Cook beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring to break up clumps, about 5 minutes.
  4. Drain excess grease.
  5. Add jalapeno pepper mixture and burrito seasoning.
  6. Cook, covered, stirring occasionally, until flavors combine, about 10 minutes.
  7. Pour refried beans into a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  8. Cook and stir until heated through, about 5 minutes.
  9. Warm each tortilla in the microwave until soft, 15 to 20 seconds.
  10. Spread a layer of refried beans on top.
  11. Divide beef mixture among tortillas.
  12. Top with lettuce, sour cream, and Cheddar cheese.
  13. Fold in opposing edges of each tortilla and roll up into a burrito.
