CROQUETTES RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 4 cups mashed potatoes
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  4. 1/2 cup grated Romano cheesesalt and pepper to taste
  5. 2 tablespoons imitation bacon bits
  6. 1 teaspoon dried onion flakes
  7. 1 cup Italian-style dried bread crumbs
  8. 1 quart vegetable oil for deep frying

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large bowl, combine mashed potatoes, eggs, parsley, cheese, salt and pepper.
  2. Then add bacon bits and onion flakes.
  3. Form mixture into patties, and dredge patties in the bread crumbs.
  4. Pour oil 1/2 inch deep in a large, heavy skillet.
  5. Heat oil over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry patties, flipping to fry them on both sides, until they are golden brown. Serve hot.
