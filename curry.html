THAI CURRY RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
  2. 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  3. 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  4. 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  5. 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  6. 2 green onions with tops, chopped
  7. 3 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  8. 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  9. 2 cups coconut milk
  10. 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  11. 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  12. 2 tablespoons white sugar
  13. Â½ cup cilantro leaves, for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Toss chicken first in 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce, then in the flour, coating pieces evenly.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  3. Place chicken in the skillet, cook and stir chicken until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and stir in curry paste.
  5. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant, then stir in green onions, garlic, and ginger; cook an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Return chicken to the skillet, stirring to coat with the curry mixture.
  7. Stir the coconut milk, fish sauce, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, and sugar into the chicken-curry mixture.
  8. Allow to simmer over medium heat for 20 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  9. Serve garnished with cilantro leaves.
