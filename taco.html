TACO RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 pounds ground beef
  2. 1 (14.5 ounce) carton Campbell'sÂ® Mexican Style Tomato Soup
  3. 12 taco shells, warmed
  4. 3/4 cup shredded lettuce
  5. 1/3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  6. 3/4 cup diced tomato

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the beef in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat until the beef is well browned.
  2. Stir often to separate meat.
  3. Pour off any fat.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Stir the soup in the skillet and heat to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low.
  7. Cook for 5 minutes or until the soup is reduced, stirring often.
  8. Divide the beef mixture among the taco shells.
  9. Top with the lettuce, tomato and cheese.
