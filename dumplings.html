DUMPLINGS RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 1/2 cup soy sauce
  2. 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar
  3. 1 tablespoon finely chopped Chinese chives
  4. 1 tablespoon sesame seeds1 teaspoon chile-garlic sauce
  5. 1 pound ground pork
  6. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  7. 1 egg, beaten
  8. 2 tablespoons finely chopped Chinese chives
  9. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  10. 1 1/2 tablespoons sesame oil
  11. 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  12. 50 dumpling wrappers
  13. 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  14. 1 quart water, or more as needed

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine 1/2 cup soy sauce, rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon chives, sesame seeds, and chile sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix pork, garlic, egg, 2 tablespoons chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  3. Place a dumpling wrapper on a lightly floured work surface and spoon about 1 tablespoon of the filling in the middle.
  4. Wet the edge with a little water and crimp together forming small pleats to seal the dumpling. Repeat with remaining dumpling wrappers and filling.
  5. Heat 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Place 8 to 10 dumplings in the pan and cook until browned, about 2 minutes per side.
  7. Pour in 1 cup of water, cover and cook until the dumplings are tender and the pork is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  8. Repeat for remaining dumplings. Serve with soy sauce mixture for dipping.
