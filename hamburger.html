HAMBURGER RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 pounds ground beef
  2. 1 egg, beaten
  3. 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  4. 3 tablespoons evaporated milk
  5. 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  6. 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  7. 2 cloves garlic, minced

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat grill for high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, evaporated milk.
  3. Then mix Worcestershire sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic using your hands.
  4. Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties.
  5. Lightly oil the grill grate.
  6. Grill patties 5 minutes per side, or until well done.
